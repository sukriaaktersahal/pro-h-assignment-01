Frequently Asked Questions

What is DevConf 2026?

DevConf 2026 is a developer conference featuring keynote sessions, technical workshops, expert talks, coding discussions, and networking opportunities for technology enthusiasts.

When and where is it happening?

DevConf 2026 will take place in August 2026 at Pan Pacific Sonargaon Dhaka, Dhaka, Bangladesh.

Who can attend?

Anyone from any field who has completed at least the SSC examination is welcome to attend.

What is included in the ticket?

Your ticket includes access to keynote sessions, workshops, conference materials, lunch, refreshments, networking sessions, and a participation certificate.

Will there be networking opportunities?

Yes. Dedicated networking sessions will connect attendees with developers, recruiters, startups, and industry professionals.

Is there a discount for students?

Yes. Students receive 40% off by registering with a valid .edu email address.

Will session recordings be available?

Standard ticket holders receive 30-day access to recordings, while Pro and Team ticket holders receive lifetime access.

How can I register my team?

Select the Team Plan then contuct us.

What do I need to bring to DevConf?

Bring your event ticket, a valid photo ID, your laptop, charger, and any accessories needed for workshop sessions.

Can I get a refund?

Yes. Full refunds are available until July 15, 2026.
